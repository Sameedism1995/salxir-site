Everything you wanted to know about Shilajit, our sourcing, and your order.

What is Shilajit?
Shilajit is a natural mineral resin that forms over centuries in high-altitude mountain rock as plant matter slowly compresses. It has been used in Ayurvedic tradition for over 3,000 years and is naturally rich in fulvic acid and 84+ trace minerals.
Is your Shilajit lab-tested?
Yes. Every batch is third-party tested, including SGS certification, heavy metal analysis, and narcotics analysis. All certificates are published on our Certificates page and ship with your order.
Which format should I choose — resin, capsules, tablets, or honey sticks?
Resin is the classic, most concentrated format. Capsules and tablets are the most convenient for travel and precise dosing. Honey sticks are the easiest (and tastiest) way to start. The Shilajit + Ashwagandha blends add adaptogenic stress support.
How do I take Shilajit resin?
Dissolve a pea-sized portion (about 300–500mg) in warm water, tea, or milk, once daily. A 20g jar lasts roughly one month. See our blog guide "How to Take Shilajit Resin" for details.
Is Shilajit suitable for women?
Yes — although often marketed to men, the evidence supports equally strong benefits for women, including energy, iron support, and recovery. Read "Shilajit for Women" on our blog.
